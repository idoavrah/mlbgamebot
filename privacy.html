Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13/03/2026

1. Overview

MLB Game Bot is a static website designed to provide baseball game results and statistics. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about the data we collect.

2. Information We Collect

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. This service uses cookies to collect anonymous information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit.

Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your "Favorite Teams" preferences. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. The only cookies present are those required by Google Analytics and Cloudflare for security and performance purposes.

4. Data Security

Our website is served via Google Cloud Storage and protected by Cloudflare. We follow industry best practices to ensure your data is secure.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. Since we do *not* collect personally identifiable information (PII) directly, your primary point of control is via your browser settings (e.g., clearing cookies or local storage).

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via the project's GitHub repository.
